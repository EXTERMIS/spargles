EDEN™ Starmap Cartographer

EDEN™ Starmap Cartographer is a simple 3D starmapping software for worldbuilders, science-fiction enthusiasts, and those interest in astronomy. The program boasts several quality of life features and was directly inspired by the long deprecated CHView. It aims to offer a simple, free, and convenient way for worldbuilders and writers to visualise their starmaps using all 3 dimensions—a task previously impossible for most. The software is currently being developed by BobTo Games.
